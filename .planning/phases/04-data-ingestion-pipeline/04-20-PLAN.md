Automate IOM Missing Migrants data ingestion with a weekly cron schedule.

This plan adds: CSV download → parse → diff-based upsert → cron schedule → alerting.
Pattern matches CBP (04-10) and UK Home Office (04-11) automation.

Output: IOM route death data updates automatically every week.
</objective>
